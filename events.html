Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for social events, and 2–3 months for large-scale corporate events.

Do you handle vendor coordination?

Yes — catering, décor, music, lighting, hospitality, and logistics are all managed by our team.

Do you manage destination events?

Absolutely. We have experience managing events across Kerala and other states in India.

Can we customize the package?

Yes, all our packages are fully customizable based on your specific requirements and budget.
