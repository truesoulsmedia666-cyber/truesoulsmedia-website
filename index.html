True Souls Media LLP (LLPIN: ACX-5488) is a legally registered Limited Liability Partnership in India, founded in 2022 by Noel George and Abin J Antony. Services include: wedding planning, wedding photography, wedding videography, event management, digital marketing, and podcast production.
