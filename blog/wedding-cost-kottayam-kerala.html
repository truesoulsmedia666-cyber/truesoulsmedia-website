How Much Does a Wedding Cost in Kottayam?

Abin J Antony

Co-Founder & Strategy Lead · 5 min read

One of the most frequent questions couples ask us when scheduling a consultation in Kottayam is: "How much budget should we set aside?" Planning a wedding in Kottayam is highly customizable, and your final expense depends entirely on guest count, decor style, and service packages.

In this budget layout, we share a realistic breakdown of wedding costs in Kottayam, Kerala for 2026, helping you plan your finances efficiently.

1. Venue Rates in Kottayam (₹50,000 to ₹3,50,000)

Kottayam features a variety of venue options, from traditional community halls to premium AC convention centers and luxury resort lawns along the backwaters of Kumarakom.

  • Traditional Auditoriums / Non-AC Halls: ₹50,000 to ₹1,20,000 per day.
  • Premium AC Convention Centers: ₹1,50,000 to ₹3,50,000 per day.
  • Kumarakom Lakefront Resorts (Destination weddings): ₹2,50,000+ rental, plus room bookings.

2. Catering & Sadya Prices (₹250 to ₹750 per plate)

Catering makes up a major part of the budget. A traditional Kerala Sadya served on banana leaves is highly cost-effective, whereas premium buffet lunches or dinners with multiple cuisines cost more.

  • Traditional Vegetarian Sadya: ₹220 to ₹350 per plate.
  • Non-Vegetarian Buffet / Biryani catering: ₹300 to ₹500 per plate.
  • Premium Multi-cuisine Buffet: ₹550 to ₹900 per plate.

3. Wedding Decor & Stage Design (₹80,000 to ₹4,00,000)

Decor rates vary widely based on materials (artificial vs. fresh flowers) and the scale of the entrance, mandap/altar, and walkway illumination.

  • Simple Mandap Decor: ₹60,000 to ₹1,20,000.
  • Standard Floral Design & Altar Decor: ₹1,30,000 to ₹2,50,000.
  • Grand Concept Decor (Glass domes, exotic floral themes): ₹3,00,000+.

4. Photography & Film Services (₹80,000 to ₹2,75,000)

Professional cinematic photography and film services ensure your memories last a lifetime. True Souls Media offers transparent, equal pricing starting from ₹80,000, which includes high-resolution delivery, premium albums, and cinematic films.

  • Basic Cinematic Package: ₹80,000 (1 photographer, 1 videographer, album, calendar).
  • Standard Cinematic Package: ₹1,35,000 (Full-frame crew, drone, pre-wedding shoot).
  • Premium Multi-Day Package: ₹2,75,000 (Full crew, multiple events, crane/drones).
