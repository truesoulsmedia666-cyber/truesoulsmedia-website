Best Wedding Venues in Kerala: Intimate to Grand

Noel George

Co-Founder & Creative Director · 5 min read

Choosing the right venue sets the entire tone for your wedding. Kerala offers some of the most diverse and stunning backdrops in the country — from serene backwater resorts and clean beachfront lawns to grand modern convention centers. Based on our experience planning weddings across Kerala, here are the top-rated venues categorized by style.

1. Luxury Backwater Resorts (Kumarakom & Alappuzha)

If you are dreaming of a scenic destination wedding with a backdrop of tranquil houseboats and swaying coconut trees, Kumarakom is the premier destination.

  • The Zuri Kumarakom: Features a massive lakeside lawn, luxury pool villas, and premium indoor halls. Perfect for grand destination weddings.
  • Kumarakom Lake Resort: Known for heritage aesthetics and ultra-premium services. Frequented by celebrities for luxury weddings.
  • Coconut Lagoon (CGH Earth): Accessible only by boat. Ideal for couples seeking traditional Kerala architecture and an eco-friendly theme.

2. Premium Beachfront Lawns (Kochi & Kovalam)

Sea breeze, sandy shores, and sunset vows — beach weddings in Kerala are increasingly popular.

  • The Leela Kovalam (Trivandrum): Features clifftop decks overlooking the Arabian Sea, offering unmatched sunset views for exchange of vows.
  • Grand Hyatt Bolgatty (Kochi): Features massive waterfront lawns, high-capacity convention facilities, and five-star luxury.

3. High-Capacity Convention Centers (Kottayam & Thrissur)

For grand traditional weddings with 1,000+ guests, convention centers with massive dining halls and ample parking are the ideal choice.

  • Lulu Bolgatty Convention Center (Kochi): One of the largest convention centers in South India, managed by Grand Hyatt.
  • Kottayam Convention Centers: Excellent AC auditoriums near Pala and Kottayam city, perfect for large community gatherings.

4. Intimate Heritage & Hills (Munnar & Vagamon)

For couples looking to escape the heat and tie the knot amidst tea gardens, mist-capped mountains, and a cool climate.

  • Elixir Hills (Munnar): Premium resort surrounded by dense forests and tea plantations.
  • Fog Resort Munnar: Great views, intimate cottages, and beautiful green lawns for high-altitude vows.
