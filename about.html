Our Story

From Kottayam to All of Kerala

True Souls Media LLP was founded in 2022 by two passionate creatives — Noel George and Abin J Antony — with a single vision: to make every wedding feel cinematic, every memory timeless, and every couple feel equally celebrated regardless of religion or tradition.

Based in Kottayam, Kerala, we have grown from a small studio to Kerala's most trusted full-service creative agency — with 200+ weddings captured, a 100% client satisfaction rate, and a team dedicated to delivering excellence in wedding planning, photography, videography, event management, digital marketing, and podcast production.

We serve Hindu, Christian, Muslim, and inter-faith couples across all 14 districts of Kerala at equal, transparent pricing — because love has no religion.
